Wongnok

Wongnok lets you search for places within a specified area. You can refine your search request by supplying keywords or specifying the type of place you are searching for.
Wongnok for Web allows you to query for place information on a variety of categories, such as: establishments, prominent points of interest, geographic locations, and more. You can search for places either by proximity or a text string.
